Blackjack Rules

Objective:

The goal of Blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Card Values

  • Number Cards (2-10): Worth their face value.
  • Face Cards (K, Q, J): Worth 10 points each.
  • Aces (A): Can be worth 1 or 11 points, whichever is more favorable to the hand.

How to Play

Deal:

The player and the dealer are each dealt two cards. The player's cards are both face-up. The dealer has one card face-up and one card face-down.

Player's Turn:

  • Hit: The player can choose to take an additional card to try to get closer to 21.
  • Stand: The player can choose to keep their current hand and end their turn.

Dealer's Turn:

The dealer reveals their face-down card. The dealer must hit if their hand is 16 or lower and must stand on 17 or higher.

Winning the Game

  • Blackjack: If the player’s initial two cards total 21, they win automatically unless the dealer also has Blackjack.
  • Bust: If the player or dealer’s hand exceeds 21, they bust and lose the game.
  • Comparison: If neither busts, the hand with the higher total wins. If the totals are equal, the game is a tie (push).
